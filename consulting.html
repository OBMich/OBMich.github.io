Consulting


When your budget is limited, and the need to make an impact is high, it is important to design for evaluation. Before you invest money and time, build a foundation for creating and evaluating the impact of your program.
Designing for evaluation does not have to be time-consuming or expensive. Understanding and documenting the social impact of your investments can go a long way in securing ongoing funding, recruiting qualified board members and staff,
and ensuring that your efforts make a real difference.

I have professional experience consulting with the U.S. Air Force, and academic experience providing technical reports to a wide range of stakeholders.
In addition to designing and conducting program evaluation and analyses, I have presented findings and provided policy recommendations to improve services and further organizational missions.
